Homing Pigeon - Animated Short Film

Fall 2023 - Present

My brother and I challenged ourselves to create an entirely animated shortfilm as quickly as we could. Homing Pigeon is a simple story about a homing pigeon delivering a love letter from a boyfriend in the city to his long distance girlfriend in the suburbs across the world. We decided to use both of our strenghts into creating this project, with my brother being in charge of the three-dimensional backgrounds, general compsiting, and assembly, and me being in charge of character design, two-dimensional character animation, and original score. Today, Homing Pigeon is the proud owner of three film festival awards: Grand Canyon University's Best Alumni Film in Fall 2023, and the UNLV Spring Flicks's 3rd Place Audience Vote and The Best Short Short in Spring 2025.

Softwares Used

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe After Effects
  • Garage Band

643 Production Logo - Animated Production Logo

Spring 2025

I was comissioned by 643 Productions, a local production company in the Phoenix film community in Arizona, to make an animated production logo for them to use on future short and feature films. With direct communication to the head of the company, we decided to build the animation off of their existing logo, a baseball homeplate. Steps taken to construct the project included sketching storyboards, designing the visuals in Adobe Illustrator, and animating and compositing in Adobe After Effects. The final product is a fifteen second clip of a baseball player sliding over a homeplate and slowly transitioning into the company logo.

Softwares Used

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe After Effects

Sunset Barbershop Logo - Logo Design

Spring 2024

I was comissioned by Sunset Barbershop, a local barbershop in Henderson, Nevada, to design a new logo for their company to be used on social media, marketing, and merchandise. The client had a distinct style he was looking at and provided references to build off of. To complete this project, I sketched out various ideas on paper, designed slighty higher quality versiona of the client's favorite designs in Adobe Illustrator, built the final design and color variations in Illustator, and created mockups in Adobe Photoshop. The final product is a sleek metalic gold logo on a black background.

Softwares Used

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop

Various Short Film Posters for the UNLV Spring Flicks - Poster Design

Spring 2025

Along with my own, I was asked by other students entering in the Spring Flicks if I could design posters for their short films Ditched by Hannah Werner and Money Line by Orell Sellouk. Each poster was a collaboration between the me and the student with multiple review sessions, initial sketches, and rough and final assembly in Adobe Photoshop. The Final posters were showcased at the event both digitally on the big screen and physically with all of the powers hung around the theater.
